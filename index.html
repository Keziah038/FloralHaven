Our Story

Traditional Floristry Since 1995

Founded in Kochi, Floral Haven brings together Kerala's rich floral heritage with contemporary design sensibilities. Our family has been serving the community with beautiful flower arrangements for weddings, festivals, and special occasions for over 25 years.

We specialize in traditional Kerala flowers like jasmine, marigold, and hibiscus, combined with exotic varieties to create unique floral experiences.
